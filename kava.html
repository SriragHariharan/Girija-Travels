Kava Island

The Kava would be an unforgettable delight for Nature lovers and photographers. Kava is an awesome and silent scenic spot located on the other side of the Malampuzha Dam. Kava is the gateway for the rain clouds to Malampuzha. The tourists come here to see the black clouds spread over the peaks. You can sit and relax and listen to the sound of small waves in water and birds. Public transport systems are very limited to reach this place. How to reach


Note : There are no direct buses available to Kava islands. The tourists should make their own arangements by riding their own Vehicle or a Taxi from desired locations

Nearest Railway Station : Palakkad Junction
Nearest Bus Stand : Malampuzha dam
Entry Fee : No entry fee.
